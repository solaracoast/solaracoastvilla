About the Villa

A Sanctuary by the Sea

Solara Coast Villa is a private luxury residence nestled within the world-class Novaworld resort in Phan Thiết. Designed for those who appreciate the finer things, the villa blends contemporary comfort with the natural beauty of Vietnam's coastline.


Three beautifully appointed bedrooms, four beds and three bathrooms comfortably accommodate up to ten guests. Whether a family escape, a group getaway or a special celebration — Solara Coast is your perfect retreat.
